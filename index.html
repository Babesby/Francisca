About Me

Award-Winning Cybersecurity Leader & Operations Director | Philanthropist & Fashion Entrepreneur

With over 20 years of experience in business operations and accounting, I am dedicated to driving organizational excellence and empowering others to achieve their goals. As Director of Operations at Slamm LLC, I lead a cross-functional team across multiple locations, delivering innovative IT training and services to global clients.

Beyond my corporate role, I oversee the Slamm Foundation, which bridges the digital divide by providing free IT and cybersecurity training to women and underserved communities in Ghana and beyond. Through initiatives like the Nimde3 App and strategic partnerships with global organizations, we are creating pathways to digital inclusion and opportunity.

Passionate about mentorship and social impact, I also founded Cheerleaders for Christ, co-founded Read 2 Lead, and continue to guide young people in public speaking, debate, and personal development.
